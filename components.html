Georgia Tech Research Assistant: Jan 2022 - May 2022

Conducted research under Professor Ada Gavrilovska and graduate mentor James Choncholas at Georgia Tech. Research topics included robot localization, computer vision, and differential drive robotics. The research goal was to develop a robotic agent to perform monocular PnP localization both locally and on a secure server to benchmark performance. The research output was a robotic snail that uses fiducial markers to estimate a pose. Details on the project can be found here.
