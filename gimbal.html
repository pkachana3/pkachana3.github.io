001 : LIDAR SLAM on MIT Mini-Cheetah Robot

Developing real-time LIDAR SLAM system and auxilary technology for MIT's Mini-Cheetah quadruped robot.

Abstract: Auxilory work to support the LIDAR SLAM system includes the development and prototyping of a custom predictive gimbal for the Mini-Cheetah's vision system. The gimbal is a specialized and cost efficient solution to dynamically offset motion blur for the cameras, and aims to use the repetitive gait pattern of the quadruped to more robustly compensate for changes in camera orientation.
