Background

Hi, I am Pujith Kachana. I am a Robotics Masters student at Carnegie Mellon graduating in May 2025, advised by Professors Ji Zhang and Wenshan Wang. My research interests include generalizable perception and multi-modal learning, and I wish to pursue these interests through research and work in industry. My current research is centered around multi-modal learning for visual-language navigation, and my research experiences include 3D reasoning, neural representations, SLAM, and efficient reinforcement learning.
